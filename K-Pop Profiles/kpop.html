Minji is a South Korean singer and member of the K-pop girl group NewJeans.
She debuted with the group in July 2022 under the ADOR record label.
Known for her deep vocals and powerful dance moves,
Minji often takes on the low to mid parts in NewJeans songs,
including "spoken rap" and "singing rap".
Her unique style is highlighted in her "killing parts" on songs like "Hype Boy," "Cookie," and "Ditto".
